Pediatric COVID-19 Cases in Florida by County July 24th

This map displays the cumulative totals for pediatric tests performed, pediatric cases, and pediatric percentage positive by county for the State of Florida. This is not the number per day. Pediatric is defined as those less than age 18. The current COVID-19 pediatric positivity rate for Florida is 14.4 percent obtained on July 24th. This is an increase from 13.4 percent obtained on July 17th. This map includes PCR and antigen test results received by the Florida Department of Health from state, commercial, and hospital laboratories. Note: map operates best on computer.

Source:Florida Department of Health Pediatric COVID-19 Report
